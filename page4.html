KPK,Pakistan

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa previously known as the North-West Frontier Province, is a province in northwestern Pakistan. It is a land of stunning natural beauty, rich history, and diverse cultures.

  • Key Features

  1. Rugged Terrain:

    KP is known for its rugged mountainous terrain, including the Hindu Kush and the Sulaiman Range.

  2. Historical Significance::

    The region has a long and rich history, having been a crossroads for various civilizations, including the Gandharan civilization.

  3. Cultural Diversity::

    KP is home to a diverse population, with Pashtuns being the dominant ethnic group.

  4. Natural Beauty::

    The province boasts breathtaking landscapes, including valleys, lakes, and national parks.

Major Cities:

  • Peshawar:

    The capital city of KP, Peshawar is a major cultural and commercial hub.

  • Abbottabad:

    A popular tourist destination known for its scenic beauty.

  • Mardan:

    An ancient city with a rich history and cultural heritage.
